# Claims and Boundaries

## Supported

- Her Algorithm is relational re-anchoring.
- The group law is exact:
  \[
  (A^{-1}B)^{-1}(A^{-1}C)=B^{-1}C.
  \]
- The law survives noncommutative groups.
- Groupoids model local charts.
- Synthetic atlas residuals detect and localize inconsistency.

## Not yet supported

- Not a complete physical theory.
- Not a replacement for gauge theory.
- Not tested yet on real physics data.
- Not automatically valid for non-invertible systems.

Best public statement:

\[
\boxed{\text{Her Algorithm is a discrete re-anchoring procedure for relational fields.}}
\]
